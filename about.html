Meet Julie!

Hi! I’m Julie — the travel-obsessed planner behind Vacay the Julie Way.

Whether you're dreaming of sipping cocktails on a cruise, hugging Mickey at Disney, or tackling roller coasters at Universal, I’m here to help you make it happen — without the stress.

I’ve spent years planning trips for families, couples, and friend groups, and I specialize in cruises, Disney, and Universal vacations. I know what it's like to want every detail to be just right — and I treat every itinerary like it’s my own.

What makes me different? I listen. I care. And I love this stuff. If you’re planning a girls’ getaway, a multigenerational trip, or a quick escape with your partner, I’ll help make it special.
