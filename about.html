Meet Julie!

Hi! I’m Julie — the travel-obsessed planner behind Vacay the Julie Way.

I’ve been married to my amazing husband Pablo for over 10 years, and I’m the proud mom of four awesome kids. Our house is full of energy, Disney singalongs, and laughter which I wouldn’t have it any other way!

When I’m not planning vacations, you’ll find me at a concert (I love all kinds of music!), catching a musical, geeking out over Harry Potter, or hanging out with friends on our next adventure. I’m a huge Disney fan and a true believer in the magic of making memories.

Being a travel advisor is a perfect fit for me because I’ve always been a planner at heart. The kind of person who builds color-coded itineraries just for fun. Whether it’s a relaxing cruise, a magical park day, or a fun girls’ getaway, I love making each trip feel effortless and unforgettable for the people I work with.

I specialize in Cruises, Disney, and Universal vacations! I treat every trip like it’s my own. So whether you’re planning your first vacation or your fifteenth, I’d be honored to help you Vacay the Julie Way!
